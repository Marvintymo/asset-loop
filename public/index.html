            🧭 Guided signet test-flip (do this before mainnet)
            

              Goal: prove the connect → co-sign → atomic-broadcast plumbing with worthless test coins.

              1. Install UniSat/OKX/Leather, switch it to Signet.

              2. Fund your address from signetfaucet.com (wait 1 confirmation).

              3. Connect wallet (top-right), then in ① below click Load my signet UTXOs and pick one.

              4. Set a price, Build & sign offer, copy the PSBT.

              5. With a second signet wallet (the buyer), paste it in ②, then Complete, sign & broadcast.

              6. Open the returned tx on the explorer — confirm payment→seller and the UTXO→buyer in one tx.

              ⚠ Test with PLAIN UTXOs, not a real inscription — audit finding #1 (inscription sat routing) must be fixed first.
            

          

          

            ✅ Routing-verified swap — one-click signet self-test (finding #1 fix)
            
Builds a dummy-padded atomic swap from your own signet UTXOs (needs ≥3), machine-verifies the inscription sat routes to the buyer output, then you sign all inputs and it broadcasts. Proves the fix end-to-end.
